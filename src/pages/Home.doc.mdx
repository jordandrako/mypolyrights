## What rights do we want?

- The right to not be discriminated against for:
  - Housing
  - Jobs
  - Health care
- The right to marry
- The right to raise children and not lose them to CPS and court custody cases
- The right to specify our partners as medical caregivers
- The right for multiple relationships to be treated with the same weight and seriousness we grant them, and not as a "kinky sex thing"
- The right to be public with how we live our lives without fear
- The social right to not be the target of assault, physical or sexual due to our relationship orientation
